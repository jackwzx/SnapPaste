SnapPaste for macOS

Privacy Policy

Effective date: May 24, 2026

SnapPaste is a local-first clipboard app for macOS. This Privacy Policy explains what information the app processes, how that information is used, and the choices available to you.

SnapPaste stores clipboard history and related app data on your device by default. SnapPaste does not upload your clipboard contents to our servers.

Information SnapPaste May Process

SnapPaste may process content that you intentionally copy on your device, including:

  • Clipboard text
  • Images copied to the clipboard
  • Files copied to the clipboard
  • Favorites, tags, and search indexes created inside the app
  • Optional privacy preferences and history retention settings

How Information Is Stored

Clipboard history and related metadata are stored locally on your Mac by default. SnapPaste is designed so that your clipboard contents do not need to be sent to an external server in order to provide core functionality.

Server Collection

SnapPaste does not upload your clipboard contents, copied files, copied images, tags, or search indexes to our servers.

In-App Purchases

If you purchase SnapPaste Pro, Apple may process payment, transaction, and receipt information to complete the purchase, restore purchases, and validate entitlements. SnapPaste does not receive your clipboard contents as part of the purchase flow.

Permissions

SnapPaste may request optional macOS permissions, such as Accessibility permission, to enable enhanced workflows like direct paste into the current app. Core clipboard history, search, copy-back, and manual paste workflows remain available without certain optional permissions.

Third-Party Services

SnapPaste relies on Apple services for App Store purchases and related entitlement checks. SnapPaste does not use third-party advertising SDKs.

Your Choices

You can manage your data in several ways:

  • Delete clipboard history inside the app
  • Adjust privacy settings and retention behavior inside the app
  • Disable optional permissions in macOS System Settings
  • Stop using the app and remove local app data from your device

Children's Privacy

SnapPaste is a general productivity tool and is not designed to knowingly collect personal information from children through an online service.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any updates will be posted on this page with a revised effective date.
