Quick Answers

What does SnapPaste actually do?

SnapPaste is not positioned as a generic clipboard history list. On Mac, it is designed as a developer workspace that helps you return to technical snippets you just used in the current task.

Why does it focus on the current context first?

The main workflow is reusing commands, code, logs, API payloads, and file paths from the app you are actively working in, instead of making you browse a flat global list first.

Is Accessibility permission required?

No. Accessibility permission is optional and is only needed for enhanced direct-paste behavior into the current app.

What still works without that permission?

You can still browse the current context, search full history, copy items back to the clipboard, and paste manually without granting Accessibility access.

Does SnapPaste upload my clipboard data?

Clipboard text, images, files, tags, search indexes, and privacy rules stay on-device by default. The app does not need your clipboard content to leave the Mac for normal use.

What does SnapPaste Pro unlock?

SnapPaste Pro unlocks advanced productivity features such as a larger history capacity, favorites, batch organization, export, encrypted backup, and stronger privacy controls inside the same app.

Purchase and Restore

SnapPaste Pro uses Apple In-App Purchase inside the same Mac app. If you purchased Pro and it does not appear unlocked, email support with your app version, macOS version, and a screenshot of the purchase state if possible.

For restore issues, mention whether the problem happened after reinstalling the app, switching Macs, or signing in with a different Apple ID.

中文说明

SnapPaste 现在的 Mac 产品定位是“面向开发者的上下文剪贴板工作台”，不是单纯的通用剪贴板历史工具。它会优先展示你当前任务上下文中的命令、代码、日志、接口请求、配置路径和其他技术片段，帮助你在 Terminal、Cursor、Chrome、Claude、Xcode 和文档之间更快再次粘贴。

权限与降级

  • 辅助功能权限不是首次启动必需权限。
  • 只有“直接粘贴到当前 App”等增强能力才需要辅助功能权限。
  • 如果不授权，仍可浏览当前上下文、搜索全局历史、复制回剪贴板并手动粘贴。
